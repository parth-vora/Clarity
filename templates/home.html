Clarity.

No matter who you are, you have to power to encourage others with a variety of esteem issues to feel uplifted and see the brighter side, even if that person is you.

Goals of Clarity

Approximately 1 in 5 adults in the U.S.(43.8 million) suffer from some form of mental illness. The goal of Clarity is to improve the mindset of our users. This is through quizzes and videos which help shed light on the truth about who you are. Press quizzes above and get started!
